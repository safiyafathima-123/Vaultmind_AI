**[0:00 - 0:45] Intro & Frictionless Wallet Connection**
*(Visual: Start on the application's landing/login page. Keep your mouse still for a moment, then begin typing the NEAR ID or clicking the connect button.)*

"Welcome to VaultMind, the autonomous multi-agent decentralized strategist for the PL Genesis hackathon. 
Right now, the DeFi ecosystem has a huge 'black box' problem. AI agents trade your assets, but you don't know why they make decisions, or if their logic has been tampered with. We built VaultMind to fix this by introducing cryptographically verifiable transparency.

Let's jump into the demo. 
The journey begins at onboarding. We want AI execution to be accessible, so we've made the wallet connection completely frictionless. As you can see, I don't even need a clunky wallet extension. I simply enter my NEAR Testnet ID, and the application authenticates me instantly, handling execution proofs securely in the background."

**[0:45 - 1:45] The Main Dashboard & 3-Agent Debate**
*(Visual: Transition into the main app dashboard. Highlight the Voltage Radar with your cursor. Then move over to the live Agent Debate feed.)*

"Now we're inside the main dashboard. The first thing you'll notice is the 'Voltage Radar' on the screen. This is our 4-axis visualizer that scores various liquidity pools based on Safety, APY, Confidence, and Market Depth, giving the user an immediate understanding of current market risks.

But the real magic is happening right over here in the live Debate Feed.
Instead of relying on one single AI model that might hallucinate or take unnecessary risks, VaultMind runs a 3-agent debate engine. 
- The **Yield Hunter** analyzes the market strictly for maximum profit.
- The **Risk Guardian** constantly monitors parameters and vetoes dangerous moves.
- The **Stability AI** tracks broader market sentiment. 
Watch as they debate in real-time. Action is only taken once all three agents reach absolute consensus."

**[1:45 - 2:45] Protocol Labs Integration ('Thinking Transparency')**
*(Visual: Point to the 'Verify Integrity' button. Click it to open the modal and display the IPFS CID signature.)*

"But how can you trust that we didn't just fabricate this text? If an AI is going to handle your money, its reasoning must be verifiable.
This brings us to our first core pillar: **Thinking Transparency.** 

Every single debate log generated by our 3-agent engine is hashed using Protocol Labs technology into a unique IPFS CIDv1 signature. By clicking our 'Verify Integrity' modal, any user, investor, or auditor can cryptographically prove that the AI's core logic logs haven't been altered after the fact. We are also preparing these immutable logs for permanent archival on the Filecoin network."

**[2:45 - 3:30] NEAR Protocol Integration ('Execution Transparency')**
*(Visual: The agents reach consensus. The app transitions to an 'Executing Strategy' state. Display the final NearBlocks receipt popping up and click it to show the block explorer.)*

"Once consensus is reached, it’s time to move funds. This is our second pillar: **Execution Transparency**.
VaultMind anchors the agents' decision directly on-chain on the NEAR Protocol Testnet.

Watch as the transaction executes. Because the AI explicitly signs the transaction on behalf of our logged-in session, we create a perfectly auditable paper trail. Here, the system provides an instant, clickable NearBlocks receipt right in the UI. In the future, we plan to shift to full Account Abstraction for this step, making real-asset management entirely seamless."
